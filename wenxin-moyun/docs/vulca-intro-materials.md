## 完整版简介 / Full Version (300 words)

### English

#### About VULCA

**VULCA** (Visual Understanding and Linguistic Cultural Assessment) is a rigorous AI evaluation platform developed from peer-reviewed academic research, with findings accepted at EMNLP 2025—a top-tier venue in computational linguistics.

**What We Do**

We systematically evaluate how 42 leading AI models—from OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and 12 other organizations—understand and interpret visual art. Our framework assesses AI responses across 47 fine-grained dimensions, including creativity recognition, emotional resonance, compositional analysis, and cultural symbolism.

**Cross-Cultural Perspectives**

Uniquely, VULCA incorporates 8 distinct cultural viewpoints, recognizing that art appreciation is culturally situated. From Chinese literati aesthetics to European formalism, from Japanese mono no aware to contemporary global discourse, we examine whether AI can navigate these diverse interpretive traditions.

**For Artists & Institutions**

- Understand how different AI systems "read" your artwork
- Compare AI interpretations across cultural frameworks
- Access reproducible, transparent evaluation data
- Explore which AI tools align with your curatorial or creative needs

**Research Foundation**

Built on VULCA-Bench, a dataset of 7,410 carefully curated samples with five-level (L1-L5) annotation, our platform bridges academic rigor with practical utility for the art world.

### 中文

#### 关于 VULCA

**VULCA**（视觉理解与语言文化评估）是一个基于严谨学术研究的 AI 评估平台，研究成果已被 EMNLP 2025 接收——这是计算语言学领域的顶级学术会议。

**我们做什么**

我们系统性地评估来自 OpenAI、Anthropic、Google 等 15 个组织的 42 个主流 AI 模型对视觉艺术的理解与阐释能力。评估框架涵盖 47 个精细维度，包括创造力识别、情感共鸣、构图分析和文化符号解读等。

**跨文化视角**

VULCA 的独特之处在于融入了 8 种不同的文化视角，承认艺术鉴赏的文化情境性。从中国文人美学到欧洲形式主义，从日本「物哀」到当代全球话语，我们考察 AI 能否在这些多元的阐释传统中游刃有余。

**对艺术家与机构的价值**

- 了解不同 AI 系统如何「阅读」您的作品
- 在多种文化框架下对比 AI 的艺术阐释
- 获取可复现、透明的评估数据
- 探索哪些 AI 工具契合您的策展或创作需求

**研究基础**

基于 VULCA-Bench 数据集（7,410 个精心策划的样本，包含 L1-L5 五层级标注），我们的平台在学术严谨性与艺术界实用性之间架起桥梁。
