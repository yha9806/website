## Tweet 1 — Hook

"This model scored 87.3."

Great. 87.3 at what? For whom? In which cultural context?

One number cannot capture whether an AI understands art, culture, or human meaning.

> "这个模型得了87.3分。"很好。87.3是什么？对谁而言？在什么文化语境下？一个数字无法衡量AI是否理解艺术、文化或人类意义。

## Tweet 2 — The Problem

The AI evaluation industry has a single-score addiction.

We compress thousands of capabilities into one leaderboard number, then act surprised when "top-ranked" models produce culturally tone-deaf outputs.

#AIEvaluation

> AI评估行业有"单分数成瘾症"。我们把数千种能力压缩成一个排行榜数字，然后对"排名第一"的模型生成文化失聪的输出感到惊讶。

## Tweet 3 — What Gets Hidden

Behind every single score, 46 dimensions are screaming for attention.

A model might excel at technical execution (92/100) but completely fail cultural context (34/100).

Average them out? You get a comfortable 63. And zero insight.

> 每个单一分数背后，有46个维度在呐喊。一个模型可能技术执行出色（92/100），但完全无法理解文化语境（34/100）。取平均？你得到一个舒服的63分，和零洞察。

## Tweet 4 — The 47D Breakdown

VULCA evaluates across 47 dimensions, grouped into 6 core axes:

- Creativity (how original)
- Technique (how skilled)
- Emotion (how resonant)
- Context (how culturally aware)
- Innovation (how forward-thinking)
- Impact (how meaningful)

Each tells a different story.

> VULCA跨47个维度评估，归入6个核心轴：创造力、技术、情感、语境、创新、影响力。每个轴讲述不同的故事。

## Tweet 5 — Real Example

We tested a leading model on a Chinese ink painting prompt.

Technique score: 88 (impressive brushwork simulation)
Context score: 29 (mixed Tang Dynasty elements with Ming Dynasty conventions)

The leaderboard showed 71. The reality was a historically incoherent image.

> 我们用中国水墨画提示测试了一个领先模型。技术分88（笔触模拟出色），语境分29（混淆了唐代元素和明代规范）。排行榜显示71，现实是一幅历史不连贯的图像。

## Tweet 6 — Multiply by 8 Cultures

Now multiply that by 8 cultural perspectives.

The same artwork scores differently through Chinese, Japanese, Islamic, Indian, Western, African, and Korean lenses.

Not because one is "right." Because culture shapes meaning.

#CulturalAI

> 现在乘以8个文化视角。同一件艺术品在中、日、伊斯兰、印度、西方、非洲、韩国视角下得分不同。不是因为哪个"对"，而是因为文化塑造意义。

## Tweet 7 — The Insight

Our EMNLP 2025 research found:

Models that rank #1 overall can rank #37 on specific cultural dimensions.

If you're deploying AI globally, a single score isn't just insufficient. It's dangerous.
