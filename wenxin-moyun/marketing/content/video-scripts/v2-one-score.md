# Video Script V2: The One Score Problem

> **Duration**: 45-60 seconds
> **Style**: Data visualization storytelling — reveal through numbers
> **Music**: Minimal piano, builds with string section
> **Target**: AI researchers, ML engineers, evaluation specialists

---

## [0:00-0:06] — HOOK

**[SCENE]** Large number "87.3" fills the screen in bold sans-serif. Clean white background.

**[SCREEN]** Just the number: 87.3

**[VO]** "Eighty-seven point three. That's the score. But what does it actually tell you?"

**[DURATION]** 6 seconds

---

## [0:06-0:14] — THE ILLUSION

**[SCENE]** The number 87.3 sits at the center. Around it, a ring of 47 smaller numbers appears — some green (high), some red (low), some yellow (medium). They orbit briefly, then collapse back into 87.3.

**[SCREEN]** Animation: 47 dimension scores (ranging from 29 to 96) spiral inward and average into 87.3. The red numbers (29, 34, 41) disappear into the average.

**[VO]** "Behind this single number, forty-seven dimensions are hidden. Some scored ninety-six. Others scored twenty-nine. Averaged together, the failures vanish."

**[DURATION]** 8 seconds

---

## [0:14-0:24] — THE REVEAL

**[SCENE]** Split screen comparison. Same model, same artwork.

**[SCREEN]**
Left panel: "Standard Benchmark" — shows 87.3 with a green checkmark
Right panel: "VULCA 47D Analysis" — shows a radar chart with clear valleys in Context (34) and Cultural Sensitivity (29), peaks in Technique (96) and Creativity (88)

**[VO]** "Standard benchmarks show a passing grade. VULCA's forty-seven dimensions reveal the truth: this model has exceptional technique — and near-zero cultural awareness."

**[DURATION]** 10 seconds

---

## [0:24-0:36] — MULTIPLY BY CULTURE

**[SCENE]** The radar chart multiplies. One becomes eight, arranged in a 2x4 grid. Each is labeled with a cultural perspective. The shapes are visibly different.

**[SCREEN]** Eight radar charts, each labeled:
- Chinese | Japanese | Korean | Islamic
- Indian | Western-Classical | Western-Modern | African

Each chart has a notably different shape. The Islamic perspective shows the deepest Context valley. The Chinese perspective shows high Technique.

**[VO]** "Now multiply by eight cultural perspectives. The same artwork scores differently through Chinese, Japanese, Islamic, Indian, Western, Korean, and African lenses. Not because one is right. Because culture shapes meaning. One score cannot capture this. Forty-seven dimensions across eight perspectives can."

**[DURATION]** 12 seconds

---

## [0:36-0:46] — THE RESEARCH

**[SCENE]** Clean data visualization. Key stat appears as animated typography.

**[SCREEN]** Large animated text:
"Framework contributes 73% of assessment quality"
"Generator contributes 27%"
Then: "— EMNLP 2025 Findings"

**[VO]** "Our peer-reviewed research at EMNLP twenty twenty-five found that how you evaluate contributes seventy-three percent of assessment quality. The generator? Only twenty-seven. Evaluation design matters three times more than the model itself."

**[DURATION]** 10 seconds

---

## [0:46-0:55] — CTA

**[SCREEN]**
Left: Terminal showing `pip install vulca`

Bottom: "One score tells you nothing. 47 dimensions tell you everything."

**[VO]** "One score tells you nothing. Forty-seven dimensions tell you everything. VULCA. Open source. pip install vulca."

**[DURATION]** 9 seconds
