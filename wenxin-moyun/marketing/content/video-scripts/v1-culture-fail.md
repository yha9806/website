# Video Script V1: The Culture Fail

> **Duration**: 45-60 seconds
> **Style**: Documentary reveal — shock, then solution
> **Music**: Tense electronic build, resolves to confident theme
> **Target**: AI practitioners, product managers, general tech audience

---

## [0:00-0:05] — HOOK

**[SCENE]** Black screen. White text appears letter by letter.

**[SCREEN]** Text: "This AI scored 95% on every major benchmark."

**[VO]** "This AI scored ninety-five percent on every major benchmark."

**[DURATION]** 5 seconds

---

## [0:05-0:15] — THE FAIL

**[SCENE]** Quick montage of AI-generated images with cultural errors. Each appears for 2 seconds with a subtle red highlight on the problematic element.

**[SCREEN]**
- Image 1: Buddhist monk in a scene with beef dishes (caption: "Buddhist monk + beef")
- Image 2: Islamic geometric pattern with human faces woven in (caption: "Aniconism violation")
- Image 3: Chinese funeral elements used in a celebration context (caption: "Funeral as celebration")

**[VO]** "Then it generated a Buddhist monk eating steak. Islamic geometry with human faces. Funeral imagery for a wedding invitation."

**[DURATION]** 10 seconds

---

## [0:15-0:22] — THE PROBLEM

**[SCENE]** Split screen. Left: standard benchmark leaderboard showing green checkmarks and high scores. Right: the cultural failures from above.

**[SCREEN]** Left panel: Mock leaderboard with scores 93.2, 94.7, 91.8. Right panel: Failing images with red X overlays.

**[VO]** "Standard benchmarks measure accuracy. Fluency. Coherence. Not a single one asks: does this AI understand culture?"

**[DURATION]** 7 seconds

---

## [0:22-0:35] — THE SOLUTION

**[SCENE]** VULCA interface appears. Clean, professional. Camera zooms into the evaluation dashboard.

**[SCREEN]** VULCA web interface at vulcaart.art. Show:
1. Image upload (2s)
2. Cultural perspective selection — 8 options light up (2s)
3. 47-dimension radar chart populating with scores (4s)
4. Red flags appearing on Context and Cultural Sensitivity dimensions (3s)

**[VO]** "VULCA evaluates AI across forty-seven dimensions and eight cultural perspectives. It catches what benchmarks miss — not just whether the output looks good, but whether it makes cultural sense."

**[DURATION]** 13 seconds

---

## [0:35-0:45] — EVIDENCE + CREDIBILITY

**[SCENE]** Research credentials appear with subtle animations.

**[SCREEN]** Text overlay sequence:
- "42 models tested"
- "15 organizations"
- "8 cultural perspectives"
- "Published at EMNLP 2025"
- VULCA logo

**[VO]** "Forty-two models. Fifteen organizations. Eight cultural lenses. Peer-reviewed at EMNLP twenty twenty-five. Open source."

**[DURATION]** 10 seconds

---

## [0:45-0:55] — CTA

**[SCENE]** Terminal window with clean typography.

**[SCREEN]**
```
$ pip install vulca
$ vulca serve
```
Then: vulcaart.art in large text. VULCA logo.

**[VO]** "Two commands. Sixty seconds. Your AI passed the benchmark — now see if it passes the culture. VULCA. Open source. Available now."

**[DURATION]** 10 seconds
