## [0:06-0:16] — THE PROBLEM

**[SCENE]** Timeline animation showing benchmarks becoming stale.

**[SCREEN]**
Timeline: 2023 → 2024 → 2025 → 2026
- Benchmark published (2023): Green, relevant
- Models improve (2024): Benchmark turns yellow, less discriminative
- Models saturate (2025): Benchmark turns red, ceiling effect
- New benchmark needed (2026): Cycle repeats

Text overlay: "The evaluation treadmill"

**[VO]** "Every benchmark follows the same cycle. Published, useful, saturated, obsolete. Teams spend months building new ones. By the time they ship, models have moved on. It's a treadmill."

**[DURATION]** 10 seconds

---

## [0:16-0:30] — THE SOLUTION: DIGESTION

**[SCENE]** Biological metaphor: a living organism processing food. Transitions to abstract data flow diagram.

**[SCREEN]** Three-layer animation:
1. **Perception Layer**: Sessions flow in as raw material. Label: "Every evaluation = new data"
2. **Digestion Layer**: Data transforms — trends aggregate, patterns emerge, preferences crystallize. Label: "Pattern recognition + trend analysis"
3. **Evolution Layer**: System parameters update — prompts refine, weights shift, strategies adapt. Label: "Context evolves. Model stays frozen."

**[VO]** "VULCA doesn't just evaluate — it digests. Every session feeds a three-layer system. Perception captures the raw signal. Digestion identifies patterns and trends. Evolution updates the evaluation context automatically. The model stays frozen. The intelligence around it grows."

**[DURATION]** 14 seconds

- **The spiral vs. line visual** is the key takeaway image — make it memorable and shareable as a standalone graphic
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 primary, 9:16 cut for vertical
- **Captions**: EN + ZH
