# Video Script V4: 8 Perspectives, Zero Consensus

> **Duration**: 60-90 seconds
> **Style**: Visual essay — contemplative, intellectually rich
> **Music**: Traditional instruments from multiple cultures, layered — erhu, oud, kora, piano — building into harmony
> **Target**: Researchers, cultural studies professionals, thoughtful AI practitioners

## Production Notes

- **The music is critical**: Each cultural instrument entering one by one IS the narrative — it mirrors how each perspective adds depth, and the combined harmony at the end mirrors the value of multi-perspective evaluation
- **Pacing**: This video is deliberately slower and more contemplative than V1-V3. Let visuals breathe. The target audience values depth.
- **Artwork**: Use a high-quality, genuinely ambiguous AI-generated artwork that can plausibly be read through all eight lenses. Avoid stereotypical imagery.
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 only (this video relies on wide composition)
- **Captions**: EN + ZH available
- **Thumbnail**: The artwork surrounded by 8 different radar chart shapes, each a different color
