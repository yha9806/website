## Tweet 2 — Pain Point

We tested 42 models from 15 organizations.

The result? Models that ace standard benchmarks routinely generate culturally offensive content.

Not edge cases. Mainstream failures.

#CulturalAI

> 我们测试了15家机构的42个模型。结果？通过标准基准的模型，却在常规场景中生成文化冒犯内容。这不是极端情况，而是系统性失败。

---

## Tweet 3 — Concrete Examples

Real failures we found:

- Islamic geometric patterns rendered with human faces
- Chinese funeral imagery used in celebration contexts
- African masks treated as "primitive decoration"

These aren't bugs. They're blind spots no benchmark catches.

> 我们发现的真实失败案例：伊斯兰几何图案被渲染了人脸、中式丧葬意象被用在喜庆场景、非洲面具被当作"原始装饰"。这些不是bug，而是盲区。

---

## Tweet 4 — The Root Cause

Why does this happen?

Standard AI benchmarks measure accuracy, fluency, coherence.

None of them measure: Does this AI understand that a lotus means purity in Buddhism but has different symbolism in Egyptian art?

> 为什么会这样？标准基准测量准确性、流畅性、连贯性，但没有一个测量：这个AI是否理解莲花在佛教中象征纯洁，在埃及艺术中则有不同含义？

---

## Tweet 5 — Introducing VULCA

So we built VULCA.

47 evaluation dimensions. 8 cultural perspectives. 130 artworks. 6 core axes: Creativity, Technique, Emotion, Context, Innovation, Impact.

Open-source. Free. Published at EMNLP 2025.

#VULCA #AIEvaluation

> 所以我们构建了VULCA：47个评估维度、8个文化视角、130件艺术作品、6个核心轴。开源、免费，EMNLP 2025 发表。

---

## Tweet 6 — How It Works

VULCA doesn't just give you a score.

It evaluates the same output through Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Islamic, Indian, Western-Classical, Western-Modern, and African cultural lenses.

Same image. Eight different — and valid — interpretations.

> VULCA不只给你一个分数。它通过中、日、韩、伊斯兰、印度、西方古典、西方现代、非洲八个文化视角评估同一个输出。同一张图，八种不同但都有效的解读。

---

## Tweet 7 — Evidence

Our research (EMNLP 2025 Findings) showed:

The evaluation framework contributes 73% of assessment quality. The generator only 27%.

Translation: HOW you evaluate matters more than WHAT you evaluate.

> 我们的研究（EMNLP 2025 Findings）表明：评估框架贡献了73%的评估质量，生成器仅贡献27%。换句话说，"怎么评"比"评什么"更重要。
