## 60-Second Pitch

### English

Every major AI benchmark measures accuracy, fluency, and coherence. None of them measure cultural understanding. The result: models that rank #1 on leaderboards routinely generate content that is culturally offensive, historically incoherent, or symbolically wrong — mixing Tang Dynasty brushwork with Ming Dynasty conventions, rendering human faces in Islamic geometric art, using funeral imagery in celebration contexts. VULCA fixes this. We built an open-source evaluation framework with 47 dimensions organized across 6 core axes — Creativity, Technique, Emotion, Context, Innovation, Impact — evaluated through 8 distinct cultural perspectives. We tested 42 models from 15 organizations and found that the correlation between standard benchmark scores and cultural competency is weak. Our research, published at EMNLP 2025, shows that the evaluation framework contributes 73% of assessment quality. VULCA is available today: `pip install vulca` or visit vulcaart.art.

每个主要的AI基准都测量准确性、流畅性和连贯性，但没有一个测量文化理解。结果是：排行榜排名第一的模型常常生成文化冒犯、历史不连贯或象征意义错误的内容——把唐代笔法和明代规范混在一起，在伊斯兰几何艺术中渲染人脸，在喜庆场景中使用丧葬意象。VULCA解决了这个问题。我们构建了一个开源评估框架，包含47个维度，组织为6个核心轴——创造力、技术、情感、语境、创新、影响力——通过8个不同的文化视角进行评估。我们测试了15家机构的42个模型，发现标准基准分数与文化能力之间的相关性很弱。我们发表在EMNLP 2025的研究表明，评估框架贡献了73%的评估质量。VULCA现在就可以使用：`pip install vulca` 或访问 vulcaart.art。

## 90-Second Pitch

### English

Here is a question no one in AI is asking: Can your model tell the difference between a funeral and a wedding in Chinese culture? Between sacred geometry and decorative pattern in Islamic art? Between spiritual symbolism and aesthetic choice in African mask traditions?

We tested 42 models from 15 of the world's leading AI organizations. The answer, overwhelmingly, is no. Models that score in the top 5 on standard benchmarks can rank below 30th on cultural understanding. And yet these models are being deployed globally, generating content for billions of users across diverse cultural contexts.

VULCA is our answer. It is an open-source evaluation framework that assesses AI across 47 dimensions — organized into 6 core axes: Creativity, Technique, Emotion, Context, Innovation, and Impact — through 8 distinct cultural perspectives: Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Islamic, Indian, Western-Classical, Western-Modern, and African. The same image, evaluated through these eight lenses, yields eight different assessments. Not because one is right and seven are wrong, but because culture shapes meaning.

Our research was published at EMNLP 2025, one of the top NLP conferences globally. A key finding: the evaluation framework contributes 73% of assessment quality, while the generator contributes only 27%. How you evaluate matters three times more than what you evaluate. The companion benchmark, VULCA-Bench, includes 7,410 samples across 5 difficulty levels and 130 curated artworks.

Today, VULCA is fully open-source. Install with `pip install vulca`, run `vulca serve`, and you have a complete cultural audit platform running locally in 60 seconds. No cloud dependency, no dataset preparation, no ML expertise required. We believe cultural evaluation should be infrastructure — as routine as unit testing, as mandatory as security scanning. Try it at vulcaart.art.

### 中文

这是AI领域没人在问的一个问题：你的模型能区分中国文化中的葬礼和婚礼吗？能区分伊斯兰艺术中的神圣几何和装饰图案吗？能区分非洲面具传统中的精神象征和审美选择吗？

我们测试了全球15家顶尖AI机构的42个模型。答案压倒性地是：不能。标准基准排名前5的模型在文化理解上可能排到第30名以下。然而这些模型正在全球部署，为数十亿不同文化背景的用户生成内容。

VULCA是我们的答案。它是一个开源评估框架，跨47个维度评估AI——组织为6个核心轴：创造力、技术、情感、语境、创新和影响力——通过8个不同的文化视角：中国、日本、韩国、伊斯兰、印度、西方古典、西方现代和非洲。同一张图片通过这八个视角评估，会产生八种不同的评价。不是因为一个对七个错，而是因为文化塑造意义。

我们的研究发表在EMNLP 2025，全球顶级NLP会议之一。一个关键发现：评估框架贡献了73%的评估质量，而生成器仅贡献27%。"如何评估"比"评估什么"重要三倍。配套基准VULCA-Bench包含5个难度级别的7,410个样本和130件精选艺术作品。

今天，VULCA完全开源。安装 `pip install vulca`，运行 `vulca serve`，60秒内你就拥有一个在本地运行的完整文化审计平台。无需云依赖，无需数据集准备，无需机器学习专业知识。我们相信文化评估应该是基础设施——像单元测试一样常规，像安全扫描一样必要。试用：vulcaart.art。
