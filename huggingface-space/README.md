# VULCA: Visual Understanding & Linguistic Cultural Assessment

**Benchmarking AI models on cultural and artistic understanding.**

VULCA evaluates 42 AI models from 15 organizations across 47 evaluation dimensions and 8 cultural perspectives. Our 6D scoring framework measures Creativity, Technique, Emotion, Context, Innovation, and Impact to provide a holistic view of how well AI systems understand and produce culturally grounded art.

## Key Features

- **Leaderboard**: Compare 42 models across 6 core dimensions
- **Try Evaluation**: Upload an artwork or describe one to see a mock 6D evaluation
- **Research-backed**: Based on the VULCA framework (EMNLP 2025 Findings) and VULCA-Bench (arXiv:2601.07986)
